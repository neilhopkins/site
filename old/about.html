About Myself

Born in Hongkong, I spent my early life in various parts of the UK and Europe as my father was a member of the Royal Air Force. Our family settled in Cwmbran in 1976 and I have lived in the area ever since.

I joined the Police service at the age of twenty two and I retired as a Police Inspector with Gwent Police in 2011. As a police officer I developed invaluable experience in dealing with people from all walks of life. As you can imagine, during my police service, I dealt with death a great many times. They were sometimes sudden, tragic and at times involved children and young people.

In my private life I have had to deal with the death of many family and friends in various circumstances and organising some. I am committed to helping you plan and deliver a service that captures the character and personality of the deceased in order to honour and cherish their life.

I am a Member of the Institute of Civil Funerals and I am fully qualified to officiate at civil funeral services.
