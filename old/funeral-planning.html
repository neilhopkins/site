Funeral planning

I offer a service that provides an opportunity for you to commemorate your own life when that time comes. I will work with you and discuss a number of practical choices that need to be made at the time of your death. This is a very difficult time for the bereaved, who are often so consumed by grief, that they find thinking about what you might have liked at the funeral service very difficult.

Life today involves many social changes, ie subsequent marriages, single parenting and non-married partnerships, etc.

Planning your own funeral ensures that the service will be exactly as you desired it to be. You can decide whether you wish to be cremated, buried or desire a green funeral, whether the service is civil, religious, non or part religious. You can decide on the content that truly reflects your wishes.

When we have agreed that the service I have captured truly reflects your wishes, a copy will be provided to you so it that can be safely stored away - often alongside your will if you have one.

This service is provided at a small fee plus mileage expenses.
