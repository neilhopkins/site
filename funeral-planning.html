Funeral planning

If you have a life-limiting illness, you may want to organise your own funeral service. It can bring great comfort to speak of the friendships and relationships that made your life so special and your loved ones can hear your own words as part of a life-centred service.

I will help you plan the ceremony; compose words for loved ones, choose music and poetry and write the eulogy. It will be exactly what you want to say. It may be the last thing that you can do for your family and will help them enormously when the time comes.

Please contact me if you would like to discuss ideas for your personal tribute and let me help you to have peace of mind.

Life's journey

Life’s journey is like a story; it has a beginning, middle and an end. Love is like a thread running through all the special events during our personal journey. It may be the delightful love of welcoming and naming a new baby. It may be the loyal, faithful love of two people joining in marriage or civil partnership. It may be the sad love of saying farewell at the end of life. At every stage of our life’s journey we are changed and our lives are re-shaped forever. Celebrancy can help you mark these special occasions with love and in a way that captures the very personal nature of your own story.
