Joining together

If you do not wish to get married in church, then you are legally required to have the formalities of marriage or civil partnership with a registrar at the local registry office or one of just a few venues licenced for this purpose. Many find this restricting. In reality, the legal part is a short procedure to obtain your certificate but your ceremony can be performed wherever you like.

Do you want to have your ceremony at a venue or location special to you?

Were you married abroad but want a ceremony with family and friends on your return?

Would you like to renew or review your vows?

Do you want to make a commitment to each other outside the formality of marriage?

I can help you prepare a wonderful, meaningful ceremony script, with vows that say exactly what you want to say to each other.

To find out more about how I can help and to discuss your individual ideas, please contact me.

Life's journey

Life’s journey is like a story; it has a beginning, middle and an end. Love is like a thread running through all the special events during our personal journey. It may be the delightful love of welcoming and naming a new baby. It may be the loyal, faithful love of two people joining in marriage or civil partnership. It may be the sad love of saying farewell at the end of life. At every stage of our life’s journey we are changed and our lives are re-shaped forever. Celebrancy can help you mark these special occasions with love and in a way that captures the very personal nature of your own story.
